Profile

Researcher and educator in intelligent robotics and automation

I work at the intersection of robotics, automation, computer vision and applied artificial intelligence, combining academic research, teaching, curriculum development and engineering practice. I currently serve as Associate Professor at the Department of Robotics and Technical Means of Automation at Satbayev University.

My professional focus includes robotic manipulator control, in-pipe mobile robots, predictive diagnostics, CAD-based design of robotic systems and AI-driven solutions for engineering and industrial automation.
